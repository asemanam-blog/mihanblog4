On August 4th SBS 'Good Sunday - Barefoot Friends' members in  new mission "My story My Song" audition. The  participating judges members in this mission are Epik High, Dynamic Duo, Tablo.

Kim Hyun Joong's real nickname is 'Kim Dog Cow' "That means sometimes like a dog, sometimes  like a cow. Today's a dog, so I will show you" he said.

Kim Hyun Joong introduced his song 'Unbreakable' with the hat dance. But this did not satisfy the judges

Kim Hyun Joong he said , "SS501 is not a rapper group, but we had song that may contain rap. When I think of it sound like Gollum. In  fact, I passed the audition without singing. Today is my the first time to audition" he said.

The judges heard that "Then, why not satisfied with your rap" and Kim Hyun Joong answered "The boss told me to do this" he confessed frankly. Kim Hyun Joong choose 'Picture' by  Park Myeong Ho to show his rap skills.
