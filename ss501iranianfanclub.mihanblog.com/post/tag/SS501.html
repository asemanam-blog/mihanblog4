Thanks to the 'DSP Festival', fans will get to see the reunions of Sechskies and Click B, but there will be no reunion forSS501 as it has been revealed that Kim Hyun Joong will be unable to perform with fellow member Park Jung Min due to his drama filming schedule.

Kim Hyun Joong's camp told OSEN on December 6, "Due to his drama and fixed schedule, [Kim Hyun Joong] will not be able to participate in the concert.  it's regretful, it would be difficult to perform due to the scheduling conflict with his filming for 'Generation of Youth'."

DSP Media will hold their first family concert in 22 years, 'DSP Festival', at Seoul Jamsil Indoor Gymnasium on the 14th with its former and current artists, including Sechskies, Click B, Park Jung Min, KARA, and Rainbow.

[News] SS501 to reunite at Heo Young Saeng’s concert [24.10.13]
تاریخ : چهارشنبه 1 آبان 1392 | 09:07 ب.ظ | نویسنده : avisa

Fans rejoice! SS501 will be reuniting for the first time in three years.
On October 26th, SS501 members will reunite and gather in one stage for Heo Young Saeng’s solo concert to be held at the Seoul AX Hall. The concert will be a farewell event for Heo Young Saeng’s fans before he enters the army on October 31.
SS501’s last group activity was during their ‘DESTINATION’ promotion back in 2010. Although they did not officially disbanded, the members have transferred to different agencies and pursued solo activities. This will be their first reunion together after three years.
