[News] Kim Hyun Joong to make acting comeback in ‘Grateful Generation’ [09.07.2013]
تاریخ : سه شنبه 18 تیر 1392 | 09:49 ق.ظ | نویسنده : avisa

Singer and actor Kim Hyun Joong will make his return to the small screen for the first time in three years through upcoming KBS 2TV Wed-Thu drama ‘Grateful Generation’.
‘Grateful Generation’ is based in 1930 Shanghai about youths who go through love, friendship and passion. Kim Hyun Joong will play Sin Jeong Tae, the greatest fighter in all of the backstreets of Shanghai who is clumsy and random at times.
This will mark Kim Hyun Joong’s return to the small screen after a 3 year hiatus following his 2010 MBC TV drama ‘Playful Kiss’. He was to return sooner with ‘City Conquest’ but had to step down as it fell through after the drama failed to secure a time slot with broadcasting stations.
In related news, Kim Hyun Joong will release his third mini-album titled, ‘ROUND 3’ on July 22.


خواننده و بازیگر کیم هیون جونگ بعد از سه سال با فیلمی که در KBS 2 پخش خواهد شد به نام (Grateful Generation) برمیگردد
