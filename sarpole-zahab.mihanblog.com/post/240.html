The Faily Kurds, who are called in Iraq, (Faily Kurds), i.e., they are part and parcel of the Kurdish people, the inhabitants of the mountains, along the Zagrose mountain range, although they are called in Iran (Lures).
They have their own Region of their own in the far south of Kurdistan is called (Luristan), which includes the regions (Elam), which is the capital (Zarin Abad), as well as (Karamanshah), which is

the capital of (Luristan).  As it is known  the (Lur) consist  of four  large phratries, they are : - the small Lur , who are ( the Failis ), and Great Lures, Bakhtiari, and Luk. There are many extensive researches about them, and about the origin of the word (Faily), which is likely related  to the word (Pahli) and (Pahlavi), too.

Note that the Lures and Failis are synonymous for the Kurds living southeast of the Iraqi Kurdistan and Luristan region in Iran, and they are a big part of the Kurdish people, and share with it  in the origin and history , language  and the struggle . Some them accused this people falsely and wanted to usurp his nationality, but certificates of history disproved these chauvinistic nationalist empty allegations. The crime of ethnic cleansing against the Faily Kurds

These crimes of Seth from the category of political crimes, but are intentional crimes ordinary serious, and be so even if the domestic law permitted, and the reference to Article II of the Convention on the Prevention of Genocide Human in 1948 shows that whole or partial groups of national, ethnic or racial or religious to kill or hinder my body or my soul or subject groups deliberately harsh conditions or sterilization and prevent reproduction or transferring children of the group forcibly separated from their loved ones is a crime of genocide, human race, which he suffered the Kurdish people by Saddam's regime and in particular the Faili Kurds.
There have been campaigns of displacement, the first against the Faily Kurds from the vanguard of the Nazi Arab first, led by Rashid A'ali Gailani, where the deportation of tens of thousands of them under the pretext of the dependency of Iran, although they are Iraqis and Iraq's population of hundreds of years as we have shown, also waves of displacement and deportation mounted after the revolution of July 1958 because of tension between the regime in Baghdad and Tehran was the victim are civilians.
In 1963 received the Arab Nazis to power in Baghdad and committed National Guard heinous crimes against the Faili Kurds, especially the so-called crimes of neighborhood Kurds in Baghdad and the reason for this is that the neighborhood of the Kurds was a stronghold of the National Movement for the Kurdistan Democratic Party and the Iraqi Communist Party and where they live when many Kurds traders which was a source of danger to a system of government of the  Nazi National Guard - in Baghdad.
But the dark period in the history of the Faily Kurds are since the signing of March in 1970 and until 1988, where the Baath regime and the Nazi-led Arab Saddam exercise of the ugliest forms of persecution and international crimes against the peaceful people who, love life and freedom, the authorities have the deportation and disappearance of hundreds of thousands of men numbered approximately one million people without reason, only that they were of the finest people of Iraq do not worship the idol, and they are Kurds, mostly followers of the Shiite sect and they hold the backbone of commercial life in Iraq and they have a big role in the Iraqi National Movement. Still more than 10 thousand men are hidden in the prisons of the regime as many of them became fields of biological experiments and chemical weapons, according to the documents that have been found, including what was done by the military unit No. 5013 of the chemical product of the Republican Guard.
Dr. Kamal in the authority of his message about the plight of the Faily Kurds, the following: the displacement of these citizens started on 4/4/1980, where families were displaced after the confiscation of all their property and personal documents (Iraqi nationality, identity card, certificate of Iraqi nationality, military service book, driver's license, the identity of the Chamber of Commerce for traders, the identity of the Iraqi Federation of Industries for the owners of industrial projects, and property documents, school certificates, university, etc.).
Then he says that Iraq's senior leadership and the orders of Saddam Hussein made the decision secret considered certain segments of Iraqi society (Faily Kurds, Persians, and some Arabs) subordination of Iranian or those with assets of Iranian and despite the fact that they were born they and their parents and grandparents in the land of Iraq, and some of them extend their origins to the period of pre-emergence of Islam. The purpose of this policy is to prepare for the Iraq war - Iran, which began in September of 1980. The total of displaced Iraqis to Iran during the period from 4/4/1980 until 19/5/1990 nearly a million people according to statistics of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent, after being accused of subservience to Iran.
